about the movie

the movie tells the life story of forrest gump(tom hanks) a slow witted but kind hearted man from alabama. there he grows up and faces many challenges but never gives up. throughout the movie he unintentionally becomes involved in major moments of american history, becomes a collage football star, he goes to war then he runs across the country even ends up running on of most successful shrimp companies in america. throughout his journey jenny who is his friend, also neighbor remains the center of his world, all he truly wants is to spend his life with her. his loving mother that never criticizes his mind, instead always supports him and teaches him not to let others limit him.
